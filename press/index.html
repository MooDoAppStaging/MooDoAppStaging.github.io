The Company

Our goal is to help you take control of your life without burdening you with complicated software. At Moo.do we believe in making powerful software as easy to use as possible. If you are on the lookout for the best tools to keep yourself on top of your busy life, Moo.do was built for you.

Moo.do is an app and website available for iOS, Android, Windows and OSX on desktops, tablets and phones that lets you organize your life as easily as writing on a piece of paper. With real-time collaboration and a content-first UI, Moo.do has been designed from the ground up to work well for a wide range of uses, from managing projects with your friends or team, to keeping your shopping lists in sync with your phone, or just keeping track of what restaurants you want to visit next.
